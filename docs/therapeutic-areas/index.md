## Overview

The BRAIN Diet addresses these conditions through:

- **Neurotransmitter Support**: Amino acid precursors, cofactors, and omega-3s for balanced neurotransmitter production
- **Mitochondrial Support**: Creatine, CoQ10, B vitamins, and polyphenols for cellular energy production
- **Inflammation Reduction**: Omega-3s, polyphenols, and anti-inflammatory dietary patterns
- **Glucose Stabilization**: Whole-food structure, meal sequencing, and glycemic control
- **Gut-Brain Axis**: Prebiotics, probiotics, fermented foods, and psychobiotics
- **Methylation Support**: B vitamins, choline, and folate for neurotransmitter and membrane health
- **Oxidative Stress Management**: Antioxidant networks, polyphenols, and reduced AGE/ALE formation
