## BRS1(FM3) - Cholinergic Function (Attention & Cognitive Precision)

## 1. Definition

Provision of dietary choline and cholinergic support relevant to attention, working memory, and cognitive precision.

## 2. Intervention Breakdown

Food-State Dominant

## 3. Functional Role

↑ choline availability; ↑ acetylcholine synthesis support; ↑ cholinergic signalling context

## 4. Mechanistic Basis (Implementation of PMs)

BRS1(FM3) is implemented through BRS1(PM3): BRS1(PM3) governs acetylcholine synthesis support for attention and cognitive precision. Together they operationalise the functional mechanism as coordinated dietary and meal-context control of neurotransmitter-related signalling.
