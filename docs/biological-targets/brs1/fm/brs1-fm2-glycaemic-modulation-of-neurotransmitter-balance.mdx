## BRS1(FM2) - Glycaemic Modulation of Neurotransmitter Balance

## 1. Definition

Influence of carbohydrate quality, meal sequencing, and glycaemic response on precursor partitioning and neurotransmitter bias.

## 2. Intervention Breakdown

Food-State Dominant

## 3. Functional Role

↑ insulin-mediated LNAA partitioning context; ↑ serotonin/catecholamine bias control; ↑ glucose stability

## 4. Mechanistic Basis (Implementation of PMs)

BRS1(FM2) is implemented through BRS1(PM2): BRS1(PM2) governs LAT1 competitive transport and meal-composition bias at the blood–brain barrier. Together they operationalise the functional mechanism as coordinated dietary and meal-context control of neurotransmitter-related signalling.
