## BRS1(FM5) - Excitatory–Inhibitory Balance (GABA–Glutamate Regulation)

## 1. Definition

Functional control of excitatory–inhibitory tone through GABA–glutamate balance, supporting neural stability, inhibitory control, and resistance to overstimulation.

## 2. Intervention Breakdown

Food-State Leaning

## 3. Functional Role

↑ inhibitory tone support; ↑ GABA synthesis support; ↑ glutamate control; ↑ excitation–inhibition balance

## 4. Mechanistic Basis (Implementation of PMs)

BRS1(FM5) is implemented through BRS1(PM6), BRS1(PM7), BRS1(PM8), BRS1(PM9): BRS1(PM6) governs GABA–glutamate neurotransmission balance; BRS1(PM7) governs GABA synthesis capacity; BRS1(PM8) governs glutamate clearance and recycling; BRS1(PM9) governs excitotoxicity modulation. Together they operationalise the functional mechanism as coordinated dietary and meal-context control of neurotransmitter-related signalling.
