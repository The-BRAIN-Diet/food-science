## BRS1(FM4) - Phospholipid-Mediated DHA Delivery & Membrane Integration

## 1. Definition

Delivery and incorporation of DHA-rich phospholipids into neuronal membranes to support synaptic signalling and membrane function.

## 2. Intervention Breakdown

Food-State Dominant

## 3. Timing Specific

Yes

## 4. Functional Role

↑ phospholipid DHA delivery efficiency; ↑ neuronal membrane DHA incorporation; ↑ membrane fluidity context

## 5. Mechanistic Basis (Implementation of PMs)

BRS1(FM4) is implemented through BRS1(PM4): BRS1(PM4) governs neuronal membrane DHA incorporation and phospholipid delivery. Together they operationalise the functional mechanism as coordinated dietary and meal-context control of neurotransmitter-related signalling.

<summary><strong>Diet</strong></summary>
