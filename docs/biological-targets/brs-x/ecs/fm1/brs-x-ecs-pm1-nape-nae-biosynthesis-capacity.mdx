## BRS-X(ECS-PM1) - NAPE → NAE Biosynthesis Capacity

## 1. Definition

This mechanism governs signalling lipids that act as neuromodulators throughout the nervous system, helping the body adapt to stress, regulate motivation, and maintain physiological balance.

It encompasses the generation of N-acyl phosphatidylethanolamines (NAPEs) and their downstream N-acyl ethanolamines (NAEs), including anandamide (AEA), palmitoylethanolamide (PEA), and oleoylethanolamide (OEA).

## 3. Intervention Breakdown

Food-State Dominant

## 4. Functional Role

↑ NAPE and NAE biosynthesis capacity; ↑ AEA / PEA / OEA precursor signalling context; ↓ diet-limited phospholipid insufficiency for endocannabinoidome entry

## 5. Mechanistic Basis

### Summary

PE → NAPE → NAE biosynthesis represents the strongest dietary entry point into endocannabinoidome biology within [BRS-X(ECS-FM1)](/docs/biological-targets/brs-x/ecs/fm1/brs-x-ecs-fm1-endocannabinoidome-signalling-capacity-and-neuromodulatory-regulation), constrained by [BRS-X(ECS-KC1) — Phospholipid & NAPE Precursor Availability](/docs/biological-targets/brs-x/ecs/kc/brs-x-ecs-kc1-phospholipid-nape-precursor-availability) [1][2].

<details>
<summary><strong>NAPE → NAE biosynthetic pathway</strong></summary>

#### (Dietary phospholipid entry)

Phosphatidylethanolamine-rich foods may support NAPE formation and downstream NAE production including AEA, PEA, and OEA → [Garani et al. (2021)](/docs/papers/BRAIN-Diet-References#garani_endocannabinoid_2021) [1]

#### (Food-derived NAPE context)

Oatmeal and other phospholipid-containing foods may elevate plasma NAPE and NAE context in humans → [Davies et al. (2018)](/docs/papers/BRAIN-Diet-References#sean_davies_oatmeal_2018) [2]

#### (Boundaries of the mechanism)

Direct CB1/CB2 receptor pharmacology is not the dietary intervention target. Omega-3-derived ethanolamides belong to [BRS-X(ECS-PM2)](/docs/biological-targets/brs-x/ecs/fm1/brs-x-ecs-pm2-omega-3-derived-endocannabinoidome-signalling). FAAH-mediated preservation belongs to [BRS-X(ECS-PM3)](/docs/biological-targets/brs-x/ecs/fm1/brs-x-ecs-pm3-faah-mediated-endocannabinoid-preservation).

#### (Integration within BRS-X(ECS))

This PM operationalises the primary dietary biosynthesis arm of BRS-X(ECS-FM1), constrained by [BRS-X(ECS-KC1)](/docs/biological-targets/brs-x/ecs/kc/brs-x-ecs-kc1-phospholipid-nape-precursor-availability).

</details>
