### BRS-X(ECS-KC1) - Phospholipid & NAPE Precursor Sufficiency

### 1. Ambition

Maintain phospholipid and NAPE precursor sufficiency so endocannabinoidome lipid signalling capacity can be sustained through endogenous biosynthesis.

### 2. Shared Biological Pool

- Phosphatidylethanolamine-rich inputs ← eggs, fish roe, liver
- Phospholipid-rich whole-food matrix ← oats, legumes, fish
- Choline-linked precursor pool ← eggs, liver, soy

### 3. Biological Importance

Dietary phospholipid and NAPE precursor sufficiency is the strongest dietary entry point into endocannabinoidome biology without pharmacological CB1 or CB2 targeting [Garani et al., 2021; Davies et al., 2018]. This KC constrains [BRS-X(ECS-PM1)](/docs/biological-targets/brs-x/ecs/fm1/brs-x-ecs-pm1-nape-nae-biosynthesis-capacity) and intersects omega-3 substrate context on [BRS-X(ECS-PM2)](/docs/biological-targets/brs-x/ecs/fm1/brs-x-ecs-pm2-omega-3-derived-endocannabinoidome-signalling).
