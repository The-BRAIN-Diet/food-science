## BRS3(FM3) - Inflammation Resolution Capacity

## 1. Definition

Functional control point governing active termination of inflammation through pro-resolving lipid mediators rather than simple suppression.

## 2. Intervention Breakdown

Food-State Dominant

## 3. Functional Role

↑ resolvins/protectins/maresins; ↑ debris clearance; ↓ unresolved inflammation

## 4. Mechanistic Basis (Synthesis of PMs)

Integrated control of inflammatory shutdown and cleanup through cytokine-tone reduction and pro-resolving lipid mediator balance.

PM4 governs Cytokine Network Modulation. PM6 governs Eicosanoid / SPM Balance.

Together, these PMs operationalise BRS3(FM3) as coordinated inflammation resolution capacity.

At the integrated FM level, this is distinct from simple suppression: resolution requires the right lipid substrate context to terminate inflammatory activity, shift mediator balance, and allow cytokine pressure to fall. Lipid peroxidation control from [BRS3(PM5)](/docs/biological-targets/brs3/pm/brs3-pm5-lipid-peroxidation-control) remains supportive here by helping preserve the integrity of the lipid environment on which resolution depends [1][2][3].
