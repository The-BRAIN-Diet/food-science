## Overview

Eriocitrin is a citrus flavanone glycoside commonly associated with lemon. Human pharmacokinetic work comparing eriocitrin- and hesperidin-rich extracts suggests distinct metabolite profiles and timing, with implications for circulating flavanone exposure.
