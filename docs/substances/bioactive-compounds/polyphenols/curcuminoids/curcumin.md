## Overview

Curcumin is the primary bioactive polyphenol found in turmeric, responsible for its characteristic yellow color and many of its health benefits. It exhibits strong anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, supporting brain health through neuroprotective effects and contributing to BDNF expression when combined with other polyphenols. Curcumin's bioavailability is significantly enhanced when paired with piperine (from black pepper) and consumed with fats, making food preparation and pairing important for maximizing its benefits.
