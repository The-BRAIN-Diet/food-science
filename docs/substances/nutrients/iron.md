## Overview

Iron is critical for oxygen delivery and catecholamine synthesis; heme iron is more bioavailable than non‑heme.

## From the paper

- Pair non‑heme iron with vitamin C; manage inhibitors (tea/coffee timing); deficiency linked to cognitive and mood symptoms.
