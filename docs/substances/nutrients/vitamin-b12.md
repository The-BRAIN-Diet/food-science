## Overview

Vitamin B12 is essential for methylation, myelin integrity, and mitochondrial metabolism (methylmalonyl-CoA → succinyl-CoA). Deficiency impairs cognition and mood.

## From the paper

- B12 + omega‑3 status enhanced cognitive protection; vegan diets require fortified foods or supplements; chlorella contains true B12; spirulina contains pseudo‑B12.
