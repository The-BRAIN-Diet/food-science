## Overview

Vitamin A and provitamin A carotenoids support neurodevelopment and immune modulation; carotenoids also act as antioxidants in neural tissue.

## From the paper

- β-carotene contributes to antioxidant network; carotenoid absorption enhanced with dietary fat and preparation.
