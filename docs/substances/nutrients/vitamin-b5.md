## Overview

Pantothenic acid forms CoA, required for Î²-oxidation and TCA cycle acetyl-CoA flux; deficiency impairs ATP production impacting brain energy.

## From the paper

- Essential for CoA/PDH function; deficiency impairs fatty-acid oxidation and ATP generation.
