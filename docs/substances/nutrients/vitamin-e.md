## Overview

Vitamin E protects polyunsaturated fatty acids in membranes from peroxidation, supporting neuronal integrity and antioxidant networks.

## From the paper

- Works within antioxidant networks (with vitamin C, CoQ10, polyphenols); food-first emphasis over high-dose isolates.
