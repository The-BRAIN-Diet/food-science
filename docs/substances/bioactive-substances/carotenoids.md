## Overview

Carotenoids accumulate in neural tissue, acting as antioxidants and supporting membrane stability; associated with cognitive performance.

## From the paper

- Absorption improved with dietary fat and optimized preparation; anthocyanins and other polyphenols interact within antioxidant networks.
