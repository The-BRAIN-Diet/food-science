## Overview

Quercetin scavenges ROS and supports mitochondrial function; isoquercetin shows higher absorption; co‑ingestion with vitamin C and fats improves bioavailability.

## From the paper

- May synergize with omega‑3/olive oil; linked to improved mitochondrial activity and energy production.
