## Overview

Genistein may inhibit FAAH, preserving anandamide and modulating endocannabinoid tone; exhibits antioxidant and neuroprotective effects.

## From the paper

- Potentially enhances ECS regulation of dopamine/glutamate/GABA; crosses BBB; investigated in neurodegenerative contexts.
