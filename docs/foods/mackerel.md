## Overview

Mackerel is an oily fish providing high levels of EPA/DHA omega-3s, CoQ10, taurine, and vitamin D, supporting brain health and mitochondrial function.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients

- **Omega-3 (EPA/DHA)**: High levels
- **CoQ10**: Mitochondrial support
- **Taurine**: Neurotransmission and calcium signaling
- **Vitamin D**: Important for brain health
- **Protein**: Complete amino acid profile (DIAAS 103-105)

## From the paper

- "Oily fish (sardines, mackerel), beef" for CoQ10
- "EPA & DHA (Omega-3): Sardines, mackerel, salmon, tuna, cod liver"
- "Taurine: Scallops, clams, dark-meat poultry, mackerel"
- DIAAS 103-105, "Omega-3, vitamin D source"

## Preparation Notes

- Best prepared with gentle cooking
- Low on food chain = lower heavy metal burden
- Sustainable omega-3 source
- Part of regular oily fish intake
