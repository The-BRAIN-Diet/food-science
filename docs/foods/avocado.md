## Overview

Avocado provides monounsaturated fats (MUFA), vitamin E, lutein, fiber, and glutathione, supporting carotenoid absorption and antioxidant defenses.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients
- **Monounsaturated Fats (MUFA)**: Oleic acid (~10-15% of calories)
- **Vitamin E**: Fat-soluble antioxidant
- **Fiber**: Prebiotic fiber
- **Potassium**: Electrolyte

### Bioactive Substances
- **Lutein**: Carotenoid for eye and brain health
- **Glutathione**: Important antioxidant
- **NMN**: Some nicotinamide mononucleotide content

## Preparation Notes

- Pair with carotenoid-rich vegetables for enhanced absorption
- Can be used as spread, in salads, or as oil
- Supports fat-soluble vitamin absorption
- Part of MUFA strategy
