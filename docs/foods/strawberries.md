## Overview

Strawberries provide polyphenols, vitamin C, and fiber, supporting antioxidant networks and cognitive function.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients
- **Vitamin C**: High content, antioxidant
- **Fiber**: Prebiotic fiber
- **Folate (B9)**: Some content

### Bioactive Substances
- **Polyphenols**: Antioxidant compounds
- **Anthocyanins**: Flavonoid compounds

## From the paper

- "Berries" mentioned as polyphenol-rich foods
- "Polyphenol-rich patterns (berries, olives/olive oil, herbs/spices)"
- Part of Mediterranean diet pattern

## Preparation Notes

- Best consumed fresh or frozen
- Part of diverse berry intake
- Supports antioxidant networks
- Pair with other polyphenol sources
