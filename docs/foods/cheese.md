## Overview

Cheese is fermented dairy providing calcium, complete protein, and in some varieties (like Parmesan), vitamin K2 and CLA.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients
- **Protein**: Complete amino acid profile
- **Calcium**: High content
- **Vitamin K2**: In some varieties (e.g., Parmesan)

### Bioactive Substances
- **CLA (Conjugated Linoleic Acid)**: In grass-fed varieties
- **Postbiotics**: From fermentation

## From the paper

- "Hard cheeses with high calcium do not raise serum LDL levels"
- See [Parmesan Cheese](./parmesan-cheese.md) for detailed information

## Preparation Notes

- Choose quality sources when possible
- Part of diverse dairy intake
- Supports calcium and protein goals
- Consider fermentation benefits
