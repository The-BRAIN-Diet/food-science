## Overview

Lamb provides creatine, heme iron, and complete high-quality protein, supporting ATP recycling and neurotransmitter synthesis.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients
- **Creatine**: ATP recycling support
- **Iron**: Heme iron (highly bioavailable)
- **Protein**: Complete amino acid profile
- **Zinc**: Some content

## From the paper

- "Creatine: Beef, lamb, pork, salmon, tuna, cod, scallops"
- "Iron(heme & non-heme): Liver, beef, lamb, poultry, fish"

## Preparation Notes

- Choose grass-fed when possible
- Gentle cooking preserves nutrients
- Important for creatine and heme iron intake
- Part of diverse protein strategy
