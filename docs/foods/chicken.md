## Overview

Chicken provides complete high-quality protein, niacin (B3) for NAD+ synthesis, zinc, and tryptophan, supporting neurotransmitter synthesis and mitochondrial function.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients
- **Protein**: Complete amino acid profile
- **Niacin (B3)**: NAD+ precursor
- **Zinc**: Neurotransmitter modulation
- **Tryptophan**: Serotonin precursor
- **B Vitamins**: Various B vitamins
