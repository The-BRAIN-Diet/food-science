## Overview

Scallops provide high levels of taurine and creatine, plus complete high-quality protein, supporting neurotransmission and ATP recycling.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients
- **Taurine**: Highest levels among common foods
- **Creatine**: ATP recycling support
- **Protein**: Complete amino acid profile (DIAAS 107-110)

## From the paper

- "Taurine: Scallops, clams, dark-meat poultry, mackerel"
- "Creatine: Beef, lamb, pork, salmon, tuna, cod, scallops"
- DIAAS 107-110, "High taurine content"

## Preparation Notes

- Best prepared with gentle cooking
- Sustainable seafood option
- Important for taurine and creatine intake
- Part of diverse protein strategy
