## Overview

Almonds provide vitamin E (antioxidant), plant protein, and monounsaturated fats, supporting antioxidant networks and neurotransmitter function.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients
- **Vitamin E**: Fat-soluble antioxidant
- **Protein**: DIAAS 47-52, lysine-limited
- **Monounsaturated Fats**: Healthy fats
- **Magnesium**: Cofactor

## From the paper

- "Almonds: DIAAS 47-52, Lysine - Vitamin E for antioxidant protection"
- "Pair with soy or legumes" for amino acid complementarity
- "GABA: Green tea, fermented foods, polyphenols (genistein), spinach, almonds, pumpkin seeds"

## Preparation Notes

- Soak to reduce phytates
- Pair with legumes for complete amino acid profile
- Part of diverse nut/seed intake
- Supports antioxidant networks
