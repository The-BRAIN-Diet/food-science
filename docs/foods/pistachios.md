## Overview

Pistachios provide CoQ10 (plant source), healthy fats, and protein, supporting mitochondrial function and antioxidant defenses.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients
- **CoQ10**: Plant source (lower than animal sources)
- **Protein**: Plant protein
- **Monounsaturated Fats**: Healthy fats
- **Fiber**: Some fiber content

## From the paper

- "Coenzyme Q10 (CoQ10): Organ meats (heart, liver), oily fish (sardines, mackerel), beef, Spinach, broccoli, pistachios, olive oil (lower amounts)"

## Preparation Notes

- Can be consumed raw or roasted
- Part of diverse nut intake
- Supports mitochondrial function
- Pair with other CoQ10 sources
