## Overview

Fish roe provides phospholipid-bound omega-3 fatty acids (EPA/DHA), astaxanthin, and other nutrients in highly bioavailable forms for brain health. Salmon and other roe provide phospholipid-bound omega-3s for superior brain delivery.


## Food Context

### Preparation

- Phospholipid-bound omega-3s for superior brain delivery
- Astaxanthin for antioxidant support
- High bioavailability compared to triglyceride forms
- Supports LPC-DHA transport across blood-brain barrier
