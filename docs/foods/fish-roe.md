## Overview

Fish roe provides phospholipid-bound omega-3 fatty acids (EPA/DHA), astaxanthin, and other nutrients in highly bioavailable forms for brain health.

## Key Fish Roe Types

See individual pages for:
- [Salmon Roe](./salmon-roe.md)
- [Lumpfish Roe](./lumpfish-roe.md)

## Benefits

- Phospholipid-bound omega-3s for superior brain delivery
- Astaxanthin for antioxidant support
- High bioavailability compared to triglyceride forms
- Supports LPC-DHA transport across blood-brain barrier
