## Overview

Clams provide high levels of taurine and vitamin B12, supporting neurotransmission and methylation pathways.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients
- **Taurine**: High levels
- **Vitamin B12**: Bioavailable cobalamin
- **Protein**: Complete amino acid profile
- **Iron**: Heme iron

## From the paper

- "Taurine: Scallops, clams, dark-meat poultry, mackerel"
- "Vitamin B12: Liver, clams, fish, eggs, dairy"

## Preparation Notes

- Best prepared with gentle cooking
- Sustainable seafood option
- Important for taurine and B12 intake
- Part of diverse protein strategy
