## Overview

Edamame (young soybeans) provides nicotinamide mononucleotide (NMN), a direct NAD+ intermediate, plus complete plant protein and isoflavones.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients
- **Protein**: Complete plant protein (soy)
- **Folate (B9)**: Methylation support
- **Iron**: Non-heme iron

### Bioactive Substances
- **Nicotinamide Mononucleotide (NMN)**: Direct NAD+ intermediate in salvage pathway
- **Isoflavones**: Genistein and other soy isoflavones
