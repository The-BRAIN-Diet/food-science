## Overview

Peas provide plant protein, prebiotic fiber, and thiamine (B1), supporting gut health, neurotransmitter synthesis, and mitochondrial function.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients

- **Protein**: Plant protein source
- **Fiber**: Prebiotic fiber
- **Thiamine (B1)**: Essential for mitochondrial glucose metabolism
- **Folate (B9)**: Methylation support

## From the paper

- "Thiamine (B1): Pork, sunflower seeds, salmon, peas, rice, lentils"
- Part of legume family with prebiotic benefits

## Preparation Notes

- Can be consumed fresh, frozen, or dried
- Soak dried peas to reduce phytates
- Part of diverse legume intake
- Pair with grains for complete amino acid profile
