## Overview

Kidney beans provide plant protein, polyphenols, and prebiotic fiber, supporting gut health and neurotransmitter synthesis.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients
- **Protein**: DIAAS 60-65, methionine-limited (pair with grains)
- **Fiber**: Prebiotic fiber
- **Iron**: Non-heme iron
- **Folate (B9)**: Methylation support

### Bioactive Substances
- **Polyphenols**: Antioxidant compounds supporting gut health

## From the paper

- "Kidney beans: DIAAS 60-65, Methionine - Polyphenol-rich; supports gut health"
- "Pair with whole grains or seeds" for amino acid complementarity

## Preparation Notes

- Soak and cook thoroughly to reduce phytates
- Pair with grains for complete amino acid profile
- Pair with vitamin C to enhance iron absorption
- Part of grain-legume complementarity strategy
