## Notes

- **Vegan**: No animal products (meat, fish, seafood, eggs, dairy, or animal-derived ingredients)
- **Vegetarian**: Includes eggs and dairy products, but excludes meat, fish, and seafood
- **Animal-Based**: Includes all foods from vegetarian category plus meat, fish, seafood, and organ meats
- Each food is a matrix containing multiple substances (nutrients, bioactives, metabolites)
- Food preparation methods significantly affect bioavailability and nutrient content
- See individual food pages for detailed information on substances contained and preparation recommendations
- The BRAIN Diet is plant-forward (70% minimum energy from plants) but can accommodate all dietary patterns
