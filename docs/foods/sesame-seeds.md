## Overview

Sesame seeds provide calcium, healthy fats, and are the source of tahini, supporting bone health and carotenoid absorption when paired with vegetables.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients
- **Calcium**: Some content
- **Monounsaturated Fats**: Healthy fats
- **Protein**: Some protein

### Bioactive Substances
- **Sesamin**: Lignan compound

## From the paper

- "Practical pairings: carrots + tahini" - sesame seeds are source of tahini
- Part of diverse seed intake

## Preparation Notes

- Can be consumed whole or as tahini
- Part of diverse seed intake
- Supports fat-soluble vitamin absorption when used as tahini
- Pair with carotenoid-rich vegetables
