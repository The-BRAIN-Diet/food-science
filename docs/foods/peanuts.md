## Overview

Peanuts provide niacin (B3) for NAD+ synthesis, resveratrol (polyphenol), and plant protein supporting mitochondrial function and antioxidant networks.

## Key Substances

### Nutrients
- **Niacin (B3)**: NAD+ precursor for mitochondrial energy
- **Protein**: Plant protein source
- **Folate (B9)**: Methylation support
- **Magnesium**: Cofactor

### Bioactive Substances
- **Resveratrol**: Polyphenol that activates SIRT1, enhancing NAD+-dependent processes
- **Polyphenols**: Antioxidant compounds

## Preparation Notes

- Choose dry-roasted or raw over oil-roasted
- Soak to reduce phytates
- Part of diverse plant protein strategy
- Pair with grains for complete amino acid profile
